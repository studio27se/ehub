Configure workspace-level settings for your team (admin only).

## Workspace Information

Update workspace details:
- Workspace name and URL
- Company logo
- Description and industry
- Contact information

## Team Management

Manage your team:
- Invite new members
- Set user roles and permissions
- Manage teams and groups
- Remove or suspend users

## Billing and Subscription

Manage your subscription:
- View current plan
- Upgrade or downgrade
- Payment method
- Billing history
- Usage statistics

## Workspace Preferences

Configure workspace defaults:
- Default project settings
- Email domain restrictions
- Single Sign-On (SSO)
- Branding customization

## Data Management

Control workspace data:
- Backup and export
- Data retention policies
- Audit logs
- Compliance settings
