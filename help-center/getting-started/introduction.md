Welcome to eHUB, the comprehensive operations and documentation software for online entrepreneurs. This guide will help you get started with the platform and make the most of its features.

## What is eHUB?

eHUB is an integrated platform designed to streamline business operations, project management, and documentation for entrepreneurs and small businesses.

## Key Features

- **Project Management**: Organize and track your projects efficiently
- **Documentation**: Centralized knowledge base for your team
- **Integrations**: Connect with tools you already use
- **Collaboration**: Work together seamlessly with your team
